What is the Vaccineapp

Vacineapp is centered on the idea of the importance of records. Vacineapp seeks to correct poor medical record keeping which can lead to miscommunication of patient data and other unfortunate circumstances.

Read more

Features

The vaccineApp provides you with various features that help keep you in good health and ahead of illnesses.

Vaccination tracker

Parents would get reminders of upcoming vaccinations for their children. Vaccinations are divided into groups. Travel Vaccinations are also factored in.

DocChat

This is a VIP paid feature (NGN 300). There are doctors on call available in App, a session lasts for 20 mins only.

Medication tracker

Helps ensure you take your medication, and will record the creation of a medication log for you.

Health blog

Keep up with health news, stories, and articles that are regularly updated.

USSD

You can be able to view your medical records via USSD

Hospital database

You can access hospitals all across Nigeria and also contact them in the case of an emergency.
